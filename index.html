Where is this place?

This place is located near kalarkahar is district khoshab and central town is manshera. It is in heart of punjab it is best for family visits.

Why soon valley?

Soon valley is the only place in punjab where it snows in winter. It had cold springs of fresh water. Even if you are not satisfied from the valley, the route to go to it is enough for breathtaking views.
